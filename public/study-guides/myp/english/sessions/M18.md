## Exam Structure
*(Reconstructed from mark scheme only — no question paper available)*

- **Total marks:** 70 (Task 1: 20 marks + Task 2: 20 marks + Task 3: 30 marks — derived from mark scheme)

  - Task 1 (Analysing): Criterion A (Analysing) + Criterion B (Organising) used for Q1e

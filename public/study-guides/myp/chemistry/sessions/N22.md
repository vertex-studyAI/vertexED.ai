**Stimulus:** High levels of anxiety about school work may cause disruption to sleep. The wellness industry has developed a number of products to help improve sleep.

If we do not have enough sleep then our understanding of situations, reaction times and decision making can all be affected.

In studies of some of the worst disasters in recent times, sleep deprivation was identified as a direct cause. Some of the effects of lack of sleep might be a reduction in communication skills, difficulty in critical and creative thinking, a reduction in self-management skills, lower social skills, as well as not being able to multi-task or make decisions.

After a night of interrupted sleep, people may reach for coffee or other drinks containing caffeine to "wake them up". Sleep reduces the level of adenosine. The structure of adenosine is very similar to caffeine. Caffeine works by tricking the brain to believe there are lower levels of adenosine in the body.

## Mark scheme conventions used

| Convention | Where used | Meaning |
|------------|-----------|---------|
| **ECF** (Error Carried Forward) | Q2d (moles calculation) | If wrong molar mass used in earlier step, carry forward error to award subsequent marks |
| **ECF** | Q4f (graph) | Y axis label from Q4e can be carried forward |
| **ORA** (Or Reverse Argument) | Q5a (research statement validity) | Accept the argument from the opposite direction if it leads to the same conclusion |
| **Accept** | Q1f, Q4a, Q4c, Q4d, Q5b, Q3e | Indicates alternative acceptable answers |

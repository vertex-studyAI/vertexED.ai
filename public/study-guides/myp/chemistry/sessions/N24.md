| Convention | Explanation | Where used in this paper |
|-----------|-------------|--------------------------|
| **WTTE** | Words to that effect — accept equivalent phrasing | Q4d (hypothesis formulation) |
| **ORA** | Or reverse argument — accept the opposite direction of reasoning | Q4d (hypothesis because clause) |
| **ECF** | Error carried forward — marks in subsequent parts follow from earlier error | Q2c: *No ECF* stated; Q2b not applicable |
| **CON** | Contradiction — if two contradictory answers given, marks not awarded | General marking instruction |
| **NGE** | Not good enough | General annotation |
| **Ignore** | Do not penalise if present | Q1e (ignore units); Q6b (ignore "proportional") |

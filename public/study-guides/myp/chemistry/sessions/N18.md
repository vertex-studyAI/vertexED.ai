## Mark scheme conventions used

| Convention | Where used | Meaning |
|------------|-----------|---------|
| **ECF** (Error Carried Forward) | Q1f (ECF from 1e "cobalt monoxide"); Q2c (ECF from 2b); Q2d (no ECF from 2nd marking point); Q6a (ECF from earlier marking points); Q6b (ECF from 6a) | Errors from earlier parts do not compound penalties — correct working with wrong earlier answer is still credited |
| **ORA** (Or Reverse Argument) | Q4b; Q4c | Converse statements describing the other compound/option are also acceptable |
| **Accept / Do not accept** | Q1c (accept "they have colour"; do not accept "hard"); Q3a (accept "acidity"; do not accept "amount"); Q3b (accept "acidic"); Q2f (accept reference to glass or sand) | Boundary conditions on acceptable responses |
| **Conditional marking** | Q4b (only award 2nd mark if 1st mark is awarded) | Dependent marks: must earn earlier mark first |
| **Markband rubric** | Q5 (19 marks, Criterion B); Q7b (14 marks, Criterion D) | Holistic banding across multiple aspects; each aspect scored 1–4 |

## Q2 — Rivers and coasts: Mekong River action plan (8 marks)

- People of Cambodia growing concerned about health of the Mekong River
- Water level decreasing every year; water becoming more polluted; fish stocks declining
- By the time the Mekong reaches Cambodia it has completed most of its journey through its catchment area
- "There is little the Cambodian government can do to improve water quality and quantity"
- **Map**: Mekong River flowing from China through Burma, Laos, Thailand, Vietnam — passing through Pakse (Laos) and Stung Treng (Cambodia) to the Mekong Delta
- **Data panel**:
  - Laos has built over 60 dams on the Mekong River; plans to build many more to generate electricity and sell to neighbouring countries
  - 60 million people across 6 countries rely on the Mekong River for their livelihoods
  - The river level has dropped to its lowest in 100 years
  - The lack of water has led to fewer fish and more algae

## Key mark scheme patterns (M24)

1. **Quality over quantity**: Across all developed-answer questions, one well-elaborated point beats multiple shallow points. Examiners reward depth, not breadth.
2. **Appraisal can be implicit**: For Q2 (Evaluate), the overall judgement does not need to be in a concluding statement — it can appear anywhere in the response.
3. **Specificity is essential for 1-mark answers**: Q3c and Q3e both require specific, named responses. "Websites" or "government data" alone = 0 marks.
4. **Best-fit model**: If student hits 2 out of 3 requirements for a markband, award the mark. Examiners reward what is correct, not penalise what is missing.
5. **Q4 Criterion A is content-only**: Format, style, and organisation are assessed in C1/C2/C3 separately. Don't confuse them — great presentation language without geographic content does not score on Criterion A.
6. **Q6 requires reference to both opinions**: Referring to only one option caps the response at 3/6 marks.
7. **Q7 requires two QoL indicators AND different perspectives**: Not meeting both requirements limits access to the higher markbands.
8. **Examples not always required**: Q1–Q3 do not require examples to reach full marks; Q4 and Q7 do — check the task requirements.
9. **Named locations boost marks**: In Q4 (tectonic script) and Q7 (megacity essay), named locations and named examples are expected for full-mark responses.
